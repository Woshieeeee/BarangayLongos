What Is Good in BRGY Longos?

For years, Barangay Longos has championed grassroots health education. Our partnership with local clinics and health workers supports hands-on training in first aid, nutrition, and wellness. The Barangay Health Center offers immersive learning through its Community Wellness Hub, preparing future health advocates and volunteers.

Youth & Community Health Program

Rooted in the values of service and empowerment, our leadership programs equip SK officials, youth leaders, and volunteers with tools to manage projects, lead initiatives, and build inclusive communities. The Barangay Longos Civic Leadership Series connects participants to mentorship, planning workshops, and real-world governance experience.

Leadership & Civic Engagement Program

Building on our strong roots in leadership, health care and education, NerdFord is proud to offer an array of coed graduate programs for women and men who are ready to advance their careers and grow as leaders. The NerdFord College Community Psychology Master's program will help you to meet your goals of becoming a Licensed Professional Counselor.

Post-Training & Future Career Pathway

Graduates of our barangay programs can pursue advanced training in public service, education, or tech. Our Barangay Longos Career Bridge Initiative links learners to TESDA certifications, local internships, and online learning platforms. Whether you're aiming to become a licensed health worker, educator, or civic tech developer, we help you take the next step.

Our Facilities

Embark on an educational journey surrounded by innovation at NerdFord. Our facilities, from cutting-edge laboratories to modern study spaces, are meticulously designed to foster success and excellence. Explore, engage, and excel in our world-class facilities.

Barangay Multipurpose Hall

Located on Pampano Street, this is the heart of community events, SK meetings, and public services. It’s near City of Malabon University and serves as a hub for barangay governance and youth programs.

Health Center

A government-run facility offering 24/7 healthcare services to residents. It supports maternal care, basic consultations, and community health initiatives. It’s part of Malabon’s push for accessible public health.

E-Waste Recycling Facilit

This center provides safe and sustainable livelihood for community workers by managing electronic waste. It’s a model for environmental responsibility and local employment in Longos.
